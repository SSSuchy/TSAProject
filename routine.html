Routine Check-Ups

Regular Check-Ups for any Condition

Instead of driving to your doctors monthly and waiting in a 2 hour line for a 10 minute check up, Omega Health can handle your check up virtually. Specialized doctors will be at your call and we will be able to remind you before your appointment. Our care packages will be there depending on what kind of condition you have. We also provide you with our app that has specialized graphs and feedback on your performance with data that you record.

Personalized Programs and Timings

Specialized doctors for your specific conditions with no wait time at your appointment. Easy to read graphs and care packages if needed.

  • Routine Check-Ups with the patient able to schedule their own timings
  • Personalized care with one on one sessions with your specialized doctor
  • Every condition available and saves you time from going to your doctor's office
  • Personalized graphs and charts for better comprehension of your condition & improvement
Healthy at Home. Healthier Overall.


What to Expect

Here is how your appointement goes after properly scheduled with your doctor:


Step 1:

After a scheduled appointment, we will remind you 1 week in advance. Then you will receive a link for the conference which will ask you to enable microphone and camera. Select "Yes".


Step 2:

Join the virtual waiting room and wait for your doctor to see you. While you wait, you can listen to our selection of music and the doctor will join you shortly.


How to Prepare

Here are some tips to ensure your appointment goes smoothly:


Gather your Equipment

You will be required to have internet access, smartphone, and a computer with a microphone and speaker


Bring Relevant Information

If you have been tracking your symptoms, log it, and bring it to your appointment


Choose a Proper Location

Attend the conference in a quiet place where you can concentrate on your doctor's advice
